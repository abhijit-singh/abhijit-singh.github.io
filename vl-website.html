Constraints & challenges

It was getting increasingly imperative to revamp the marketing website but a complete re-do was impossible because of the lack of dev resources and increasing pressure to realease new features. A quick overhaul was needed without disturbing the development pipeline.

We then decided to keep the stay with the wordpress platform & theme, and make all possible design upgradations. I took the responsibility to drive the project. A lot of time was spent getting familiarized with the wordpress theme but once that was done, understanding the limitations and making changes within the spectrum of what was allowed in wordpress became an easier process.
