What I've been upto

For the past couple of years I've been working at Vacation Labs, designing a product to solve the challenges, and automate the everyday activities of a travel business. Having joined Vacation Labs right out of college as a design intern, I got the opportunity to collaborate with some great designers, developers, and the founder himself. Eventually taking full responsibility of design for the company exposed me to the practicalities of building a product, which not only expanded my design skills, but also made me aware of the blanket of development and sales activities that are vital to its success.


When I'm not working, you'll find me capturing mundane yet fascinating landscapes, climbing a mountain, or winning a game of FIFA. To know more about me, discuss an idea that'll transform the world, plan a trek, or even play FIFA, feel free to reach out.
