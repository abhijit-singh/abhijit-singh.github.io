WHEN

COMPANY

Vacation Labs

MY ROLE

Product Design & Research

TEAM

2 UX Designers

In 2018, Vacation Labs intended to deliver an all-in-one solution for travel operators. Already having a Travel Website Builder, Booking Engine and Distribution System in their arsenal, we were tasked with figuring out missing components and fill any possible gaps. We started by talking to multiple small & medium scale travel operators & agents to understand their business and help address their concerns. Over the course of the next 6 months, we designed and delivered two major solutions coupled with multiple smaller features that helped Vacation Labs’ existing customers streamline their business and also expanded their target market, enabling the acquisition of a sizeable number of new customers.
