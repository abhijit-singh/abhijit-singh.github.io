Humain Health B2B Apps

Appointment scheduling, sample tracking, virtual doctor consultation & medical record managment applications for the Indian metropolitan consumers.

Summary

WHEN

2019

COMPANY

SigTuple, Ittisa

MY ROLE

Product Design Lead, Project Management

TEAM

2 UX Designers, 1 Visual Designer, 2 Product Analysts

Humain Health, a Sigtuple brand, has diagnostic laboratories in multiple cities accross India. In August 2019, Humain Health started providing healthcare services directly to customers through it’s Customer Experiences Centres. To enable this, we built a Web Portal as well as a dedicated mobile app, integrated with Humain’s Lab and Clinic Management software, that allowed end customers to order diagnostic tests & packages, book virtual & in-person appointments, and also store & access medical records on-the-go. In spite of slow adoption, these tools were instrumental for Humain Health to scale up their customer facing business.
