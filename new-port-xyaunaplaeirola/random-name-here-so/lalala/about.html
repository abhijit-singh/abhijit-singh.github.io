I am Abhijit, a product designer from India.

I’m currently handling Product Design at Sigtuple, crafting experiences for a cluster of tools to manage diagnostic laboratories and designing interfaces for multiple AI-powered diagnostic devices. Over the past two years, I’ve worked with doctors, lab technicians, phlebotomists and other medical professionals to understand the ins & outs of the diagnostic healthcare ecosystem. I also got the opportunity to work remotely with incredible design & development agencies like Grappus, The Minimalist and Ittisa, collaborating on and leading several design projects.

Having been fascinated by softwares like Photoshop and Corel Draw since 8th grade, I’ve now been designing interfaces for 7+ years, with a professional experience of 4+ years. My background in Computer Science Engineering has allowed me to have a deeper technical understanding of how products are built and allowed me to look at things from the developers’ perspective.
