Trabug - Custom OS

Trabug is a custom mobile operating system, designed to be used by international travellers during their journey to India, giving them easy access to all relevant information ranging from best places to visit, to more basic needs like booking a taxi, exchanging currency, calling emergency services and much more.

  • Product Website

My Role

My major contributions were to the UI & branding of the product. I was responsible for making a set of vital flows, starting with the splash screens to greet & onboard users, handcrafting the navigation UX to provide a layer of simplification over the stock Android OS, and finally contributing to the final visual design of the Home Screen. Beyond the operating system, I made the logo and also handled the package design for Trabug.
