Giving business owners access to intelligent, actionable reports & charts for their website, bookings, and customer satisfaction.

  • Role

    UX, Visual

  • When

Project Summary

Studying the end user requirements and creating a summary of useful data that is easy to consume as well as action. As each business owner needed a different set of operational statistics, the challenge was to unify highly varying requirements into meaningful data, which can impact the overall productivity & highlight any inefficient components of the system.

The most important step in this project was to evaluate what data made sense to the business owner and what was the respective actionable for it. Only a handful of values needed to be narrowed down from a set of over 100 data points.
