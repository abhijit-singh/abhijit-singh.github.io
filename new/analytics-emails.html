Analytics Emails

Daily, weekly, and monthly emails for business owners using the the Vacation Labs System, to monitor & improve their operating efficiency.

My Role

Studying the end user requirements and creating a summary of useful data that is easy to consume as well as action. As each business owner needed a different set of operational statsitics, the challenge was to unify highly varying requirements into meaningful data, which can impact the overall productivity & highlight any inefficient components of the system.

Uncovering & Validating Requirements

It was very important to understand what data made sense to the business owner and what was the respective actionable for it. Only a handful of values needed to be narrowed down from a set of over 100 data points.

Mockups

After choosing the data sets & the appropriate representation for each, high fidelity mockups were designed & handed over for development.

Other Details

Team Size – 1 Designer, 1 Developer
Project Duration – 4 Weeks(2 for Design, 3 for Development & Testing)
