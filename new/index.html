What I've Been Upto

For the past couple of years I've been working at Vacation Labs, designing a sales & management product for the travel industry. Having joined Vacation Labs right out of college as a design intern, I got the opportunity to collaborate with some great designers, developers & and the founder himself. Eventually taking full responsibility of design for the company exposed me to the practicalities of building a product, which not only expanded my design skills, but also made me more aware of the blanket of development & sales activities that are vital to it’s success.
