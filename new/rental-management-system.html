Rental Management System

A new branch of the Vacation Labs system, this allows travel business owners to effectively market & sell, as well as effectively manage their rental inventory. Catering to all kind of rental models - Taxi Rentals, Point to Point Rentals, Self-Drive Rentals, Equipment Rentals, and Accomodation Rentals, the addition of this branch approximatey doubles the target market for the company.

My Role

I was responsible for the overall conceptualization & design for the project, beginning with the most basic market research & requirement analysis, to finally producing the full blown prototype & designs. As of June 2017, the project is under development and is all set to be the next big addition to the Vacation Labs product. Watch this space for the detailed Case Study on this project after it goes live.
