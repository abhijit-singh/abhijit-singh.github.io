Rental Management System

Designed a new branch of the Vacation Labs system, allowing travel business owners to effectively market, sell, and manage their Rental Inventory. Catering to all kind of rental models - Taxi Rentals, Point to Point Rentals, Self-Drive Rentals, Equipment Rentals, and Accomodation Rentals, the addition of this branch aims to double the target market for the company.
