Travel Website Builder

Empowering tour operators & agents with a tool to easily create a travel optimized website with and sell their products using an integrated booking engine.

  • Role

    IA, UX, Visual

  • When

    Jan 2016 – April 2016

  • Platform

    Web App

  • Team

    3 Designers, 5 Devs
View Live Product →

My Role

The project was handed to the design team after the market research & requirement analysis had been done. The major challenge was to make the Admin UI easy enough even for a non tech-savvy user, and at the same time, allow the user to tweak an extensive number of options to mirror their visual & functional expectations in their website. We started with information architecture & user flows, then came wireframing and low fidelity prototypes, and finally the high fidelity mockups along with an Invision prototype to illustrate the user flows was handed over for development.

A mindmap was created with a broad level view of the entire admin system.

User Cases & Sketches

Every possible business use case was listed, studied & compared. We interacted with a handful of selected clients to understand their perspective on the user flow. Quick & dirty sketches were made to ensure that the system will be able to handle every case elegantly.

Wireframes

The rough ideas were then given shape & added to the existing app. A quick prototype was also made to assure all the flows were in order. Tool used - Balsamiq Mockups.

Mockups & Final Prototype

High fidelity mockups were finally created using Sketch & were converted into a clickable prototype using Invision.
